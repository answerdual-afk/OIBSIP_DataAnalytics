## Insights and Recommendations

### 1. Customer Age Trend

**Insight:**
Most customers belong to the middle-age group, with an average age of around 41 years.

**Recommendation:**
The business can design products and marketing strategies targeted toward middle-aged customers.

### 2. Gender Distribution

**Insight:**
The customer base is almost evenly distributed between male and female customers.

**Recommendation:**
The company should maintain balanced marketing campaigns instead of focusing heavily on one gender.

### 3. Product Category Performance

**Insight:**
Electronics generated the highest revenue, while Beauty products generated the lowest sales revenue.

**Recommendation:**
The company should continue investing in Electronics while improving promotions and discounts for Beauty products.

### 4. Purchase Behavior

**Insight:**
Customers purchased Clothing more frequently, but Electronics generated more revenue due to higher prices.

**Recommendation:**
The business can introduce premium offerings in Clothing to improve revenue generation.

### 5. Monthly Sales Trend

**Insight:**
Sales varied across months, indicating seasonal purchasing behavior.

**Recommendation:**
The company should prepare inventory and marketing campaigns before high-sales months and introduce promotions during low-sales months.
